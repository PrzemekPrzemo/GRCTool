ETHICS AND ANTI-CORRUPTION POLICY
BY PRZEMYSŁAW SZULECKI, CSO
Zero tolerance for bribery and corruption. Gift and entertainment thresholds, cultural exceptions (KSA/Nazaha, USA/FCPA, PL/EU), and protected reporting channels.
TSH-SEC-POL-018  |  v1.0  |  2026-07-14  |  CSO — Przemysław Szulecki
# 1. Zero Tolerance
TSH applies zero tolerance to bribery and corruption in any form. This policy covers all employees, contractors, sub-contractors and third parties acting on TSH's behalf, regardless of jurisdiction.
# 2. Gift and Entertainment Thresholds
External gifts received/given: up to 150 PLN / 50 EUR / 50 USD per single instance requires no approval. Above this threshold requires Management Board approval.

Business entertainment: must be reasonable and customary, documented in every case.

Cultural exceptions: local regulations are taken into account — KSA: reference to Nazaha (CIB, nazaha.gov.sa); USA: FCPA; PL/EU: anti-corruption law.
# 3. Reporting Channels and Whistleblower Protection
Anonymous channel: security@tsh.io. Aligned with EU Whistleblower Directive 2019/1937 — no retaliation for good-faith reports. Reports reviewed by CSO + Management Board.

All third parties must meet equivalent standards: due diligence under POL-006, anti-bribery clauses in contracts.
