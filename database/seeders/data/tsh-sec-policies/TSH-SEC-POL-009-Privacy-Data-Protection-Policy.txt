PRIVACY & DATA PROTECTION POLICY
BY PRZEMYSŁAW SZULECKI, CSO
TSH's commitment to GDPR and PDPL compliance. Covers processor obligations for client project data, controller obligations for HR data, and breach notification timelines.
TSH-SEC-POL-009  |  v2.0  |  2025-01-01  |  CSO — Przemysław Szulecki
# 1. Purpose & Scope
This policy defines TSH's commitment to protecting the personal data of employees, clients, and individuals whose data TSH processes. TSH operates under GDPR (EU) as both a Controller (own HR/marketing data) and a Processor (client project data). KSA PDPL applies to data of Saudi residents.
# 2. Data Protection Principles (GDPR Art. 5)
# 3. TSH as Data Processor (Client Projects)
When TSH processes personal data on behalf of a client, TSH acts as a Data Processor. Requirements:
Data Processing Agreement (CLT-403) in place before any processing begins
Process only on documented client instructions
Implement appropriate technical and organisational measures (this ISMS)
Assist client with Data Subject Rights requests within 30 days
Notify client of personal data breach within 24 hours (to support their 72-hour GDPR obligation)
Delete or return all personal data within 30 days of project close
Sub-processors listed in DPA Annex 3; client notified of changes with 30 days notice
# 4. Legal Bases — TSH as Controller
# 5. Cross-Border Transfers
EU personal data: processed in EU by default (Google EU region, Microsoft EU region)
Transfers outside EEA: Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) in all relevant vendor DPAs
KSA personal data (PDPL): not transferred outside KSA without SDAIA approval or applicable exception
UK personal data: UK SCCs or adequacy decision applies
# 6. Breach Notification
Personal data breaches follow PROC-205 (Data Breach Notification Procedure). Key timelines:
Client notification: within 24 hours of TSH becoming aware (to support client's 72h GDPR obligation)
UODO notification: within 72 hours if risk to data subjects' rights and freedoms
Data subject notification: without undue delay if high risk
SDAIA (KSA): within 72 hours for Saudi residents' data
# 7. Data Subject Rights
TSH respects and facilitates all data subject rights: access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, objection. Requests responded to within 30 days. Contact: privacy@tsh.io.
