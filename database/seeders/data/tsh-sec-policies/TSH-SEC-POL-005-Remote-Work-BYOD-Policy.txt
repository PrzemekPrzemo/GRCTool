REMOTE WORK & BYOD POLICY
BY PRZEMYSŁAW SZULECKI, CSO
TSH is ~90% remote. Device requirements: IRU for all Apple devices (MacBook/iPhone/iPad), Intune for Windows. BYOD via IRU MAM. Network security, physical requirements, and incident reporting.
TSH-SEC-POL-005  |  v2.2  |  2025-01-01  |  CSO — Przemysław Szulecki
# 1. Purpose & Scope
TSH operates as a ~90% remote company. This policy defines security requirements for remote work, home offices, co-working spaces, and personal device (BYOD) use. Applies to all TSH personnel globally.
# 2. Device Requirements
## 2.1 Company-Issued Devices
IRU is TSH's Apple device management platform. All Apple devices — MacBooks, iPhones, iPads — are managed exclusively through IRU. Do not attempt to enroll Apple devices in any other MDM system.
## 2.2 Personal Devices (BYOD)
Personal devices may only access TSH Confidential data after enrollment in the appropriate platform:
macOS personal: IRU user enrollment (limited scope — work data isolated, personal data untouched)
iOS/iPadOS personal: IRU App Protection Policy (MAM without full enrollment)
Windows personal: Microsoft Intune MAM enrollment
Android personal: Microsoft Intune MAM work profile
BYOD devices never used for RESTRICTED data without explicit CSO approval
# 3. Network Security
## 3.1 Home Networks
WPA3 or WPA2-AES minimum — WEP and WPA (TKIP) prohibited
Router default password must be changed
Dedicated work SSID recommended (separate from IoT/family devices)
No VPN required for standard corporate apps — Entra ID Conditional Access enforces MFA + device compliance
## 3.2 Public Wi-Fi
Always-on VPN required when using public Wi-Fi (airports, hotels, cafes)
Mobile hotspot preferred over untrusted public networks
VPN policy enforced by IRU (Apple) and Intune (Windows) MDM profiles
# 4. Physical Security at Remote Locations
Screen lock within 5 minutes of inactivity — enforced by IRU/Intune
Privacy screen recommended in public spaces
Laptop never left unattended in public — theft reported to IT within 1 hour
Sensitive calls and video meetings conducted in private — headphones in public
Printed Confidential documents: cross-cut shredded when no longer needed
# 5. Incident Reporting
Report immediately to IT Lead (it@tsh.io) and CSO (cso@tsh.io):
Lost or stolen device — IRU (Apple) or Intune (Windows) remote wipe initiated immediately
Suspected compromise of any work account
Lost/stolen device: call IT Lead immediately. IRU remote wipe will be activated within minutes. Time is critical — act within 1 hour of discovering the loss.
