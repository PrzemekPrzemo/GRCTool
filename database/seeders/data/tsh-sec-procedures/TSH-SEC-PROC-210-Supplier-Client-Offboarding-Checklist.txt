SUPPLIER & CLIENT PROJECT OFFBOARDING CHECKLIST
BY PM / IT LEAD
Security checklist for closing every client project or supplier engagement. Covers access revocation, credential rotation, data deletion, and deliverable confirmation.
TSH-SEC-PROC-210  |  v2.0  |  2025-01-01  |  CSO — Przemysław Szulecki
# 1. Purpose
This checklist ensures secure closure of every client project or supplier engagement. Complete for every project regardless of size. See PROC-211 for employee/contractor personal offboarding.
